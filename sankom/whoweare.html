Our main goal

In July 1990, Marija Kozin established family business SAN.KO.M. d.o.o. . Depending on her previous years of experience and contacts with the clients, she noticed a serious shortage of certain medical devices for the control of sterility of instruments and other materials. So she started to manufacture indicators that are based on color change, that visually immediately show whether the material has been in the process of sterilization. Soon she added to the program manufacturing of reels and pouches for sterilization and also expanded the sales range with articles of gauze, which the market was heavily lacking at the time.

Due to the rising volume of production and sales, the whole family soon became involved in the business. We also hired a few assistants - operators of the production machines and for delivery services. In 1996 we purchased a new manufacturing facility, which we slowly arranged and expanded until 2000. After 2000, the part of the team shifted to a more professional - technical waters and began to carry out validations and development of a new program for the control of sterilization processes in hospitals. Due to constant growth and space requirements, we have gained a new warehouse in the year of 2007. We followed the idea to improve traceability of control of sterilization processes in hospitals constantly and so we customized program for the Slovenian market. By developing it, we have found out that we need to constantly keep up with new requirements of modern procedures of control in sterilization according to ISO standards and thus also be focused on the development of new alternatives for the supervision and control of sterilization.

Today we have a development team of four innovators who look into the future and with whom we want to help personel that is responsible for the proper and well-conducted sterilization procedures, at home and abroad . With our products we supply domestic and foreign markets. For better and faster response to demands in area of former Yugoslavia, we opened another subsidiary company in Macedonia, early in the year of 2014. We continue our own way globally. But our first priority always remains quality production of products, quality service and satisfied customer or user.
