4Q VALIDATION AND MANAGEMENT CONSULTING

Quality generates trust and security

4Q always starts be listing to their customers in order to understand the specific requirements of the SD staff.

Validation of Sterilization and disinfection processes are far more than the measurement of physical and chemical parameters of the different sterilization processes.

4Q guarantees complete validation of your SD equipment based on objective measurements and synthesis of interdisciplinary analyses of validated processes.

4Q Is aware of the fact that objective measurement validations has a subjective evaluation of assurance level of your validation process made by experienced 4Q validators.

4Q Validators are able to point out weak links in the different reprocessing chain providing information and corrections they will educate and include the SD people in the total validated process.

4Q provides validation services for the complete reprocessing chain in your SD from design qualification to the periodic performance qualification.

4Q validations of sterilization processes are multidisciplinary and includes parametric measurements, microbiological and chemical analyzing as well as simulating of real world processes in laboratory conditions.

4Q Is constantly researching for threats and unexplained phenomena in the discipline of sterilization processes, which is putting us to a higher level of understanding and expertise.

We are validating according to norms EN ISO14937, EN ISO 17665, EN ISO15883 etc.

We continue our own way globally. But our first priority always remains quality production of products,quality service and satisfied customer or user.
